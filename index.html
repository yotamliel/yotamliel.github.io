Yotam Liel

Postdoctoral Research Fellow, Cornell Tech

Cornell University, New York

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Cornell Tech, Cornell University, working with Prof. Mor Naaman as part of the Social Technologies Lab. My research examines how people interact with algorithmic systems—particularly how AI recommendations influence human judgment, decision-making, and behavior.

I completed my Ph.D. at Tel Aviv University, where my dissertation “Turning Off Your Better Judgment – Conformity to Algorithmic Recommendations” investigated how and why people conform to algorithmic advice across different contexts.

My work spans algorithmic conformity, human–AI collaboration, AI-mediated communication, and the behavioral implications of algorithmic management and supervision.
